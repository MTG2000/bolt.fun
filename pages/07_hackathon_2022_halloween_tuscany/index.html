Join us at the first edition of the "Lightning Halloween", a seasonal event aimed at promoting development on the Lightning Network and driving Bitcoin adoption!

The event is the "developer track" of the Lugano Plan B Forum and will take place immediately after the Forum, with free transfer from Lugano for attendees of the 1st event.

Held in Viareggio, just a short train ride away from most major attractions in Tuscany, the first ever Italian LN meetup will feature world-class experts, who will deliver speeches on some of the most relevant and current topics within the Lightning Network ecosystem.

After the talks, participants will be able to work together on their preferred projects and participate in practical workshops led by industry experts and fellow Bitcoiners.

As if that weren’t exciting enough, participants will have a chance to meet fellow Bitcoin and Lightning Network developers and designers and team up in sight of the global hackathon "Legends of Lightning". In order to provide participants with the best possible experience, only a limited number of places will be available, to ensure that everybody will have enough time to ask all their questions to speakers and mentors.
